About This Tool

This retirement planning tool uses Monte Carlo simulation to model the uncertainty of investment returns and inflation. For each simulation, it randomly selects historical S&P 500 returns and inflation rates from the past 100 years and applies them to each year of your retirement period. By running thousands of these randomized scenarios, it helps you understand the probability of your retirement plan succeeding under different market conditions.

Understanding Success Rate

The success rate shows the percentage of simulations where your portfolio lasted throughout your entire retirement period. A higher percentage indicates a more robust retirement plan. Financial experts often recommend aiming for at least a 75-80% success rate.

About This Chart

This chart shows how the probability of success changes over time. The declining curve is normal - the further into the future you look, the more uncertainty exists. The reference lines indicate different risk levels: 90% (very safe), 75% (safe), and 50% (risky).
